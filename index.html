The Art of Asking: A Prompt Engineering Guide

🎯 Be Clear and Specific

Avoid ambiguity. Instead of "Write an email," say "Write a concise, persuasive cold email introducing our new marketing automation platform to small business owners."

CONTEXT Provide Context

Give the AI background information. Include details about your company, the target audience, or the desired outcome to get a more relevant response.

FORMAT Define the Desired Format

Specify the output structure. Ask for bullet points, an email draft, a summary paragraph, or any other format you need.

🎭 Set the Tone/Persona

Guide the AI's style. Use phrases like "Write in a professional yet approachable tone," or "Act as a seasoned sales expert."

🔄 Iterate and Refine

Don't settle for the first response. Think of it as a conversation. If the output isn't perfect, provide follow-up instructions to guide the AI toward a better result.

Responsible AI: Your Co-Pilot, Not a Replacement

Human in the Loop

AI is your co-pilot. Always review, edit, and personalize AI-generated content. Your expertise and relationship-building skills are irreplaceable.

Accuracy & "Hallucinations"

AI models can provide incorrect information. Always fact-check crucial details, statistics, and client-specific claims before using them.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Adhere strictly to Meta's internal guidelines. Avoid sharing sensitive customer data or proprietary information in prompts.

Ethical Considerations

Be mindful of potential biases in AI outputs. Ensure all generated content is fair, inclusive, and avoids any discriminatory language.

AI Key Terms for Sales Professionals

Familiarize yourself with these essential AI terms to better understand and leverage the power of artificial intelligence in your sales activities.

Generative AI

AI that can create new content (text, images, audio, etc.) based on patterns learned from vast datasets. For sales: Generates personalized emails, ad copy, presentation outlines.

Large Language Model (LLM)

A type of generative AI specifically designed to understand and generate human language. Trained on massive amounts of text data. For sales: The core engine behind drafting messages, summarizing documents, and more.

Prompt Engineering

The art and science of crafting effective inputs (prompts) for AI models to get the desired output. For sales: Directly impacts the quality and relevance of generated sales collateral and communication.

Machine Learning (ML)

A subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data without explicit programming, making predictions or decisions. For sales: Powers capabilities like lead scoring, predictive analytics, and sales forecasting.

AI Ethics

Principles guiding the responsible development and use of AI, ensuring fairness, transparency, accountability, and privacy. For sales: Crucial for maintaining customer trust and compliance, especially with personalized outreach and data usage.

Hallucination

When an AI model generates incorrect, nonsensical, or made-up information, presented as factual. For sales: Highlights the critical need for human review and fact-checking of all AI-generated content before use.

Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)

An approach where human intelligence is integrated into AI processes to review, validate, or refine AI outputs. For sales: Emphasizes that AI is a powerful co-pilot, always requiring human oversight for quality control, personalization, and strategic decision-making.

Exploring AI Models: Your Sales Toolkit

Different AI models have unique strengths and primary integrations. Understanding these can help you choose the best tool for a specific sales task.

Meta AI (and Llama)

Meta AI refers to the suite of AI experiences and features embedded across Meta's platforms (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp). It's powered by underlying models like Llama (Large Language Model Meta AI), which Meta has also made open-source.

Relevant Sales Use Cases:
  • Content Generation & Social Engagement: Generate content suggestions, captions, and hashtags for social media posts on Meta platforms. Automate responses in DMs (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp) for customer service and initial inquiries.
  • Personalized Campaigns: Analyze user preferences on Meta platforms to tailor ad content and messaging for hyper-personalized campaigns.
  • Community Management: Assist with managing large communities, identifying trends in conversations, and moderating content on Meta's social platforms.
  • Creative Inspiration: Storyboarding and creative inspiration for ad creatives or visual content, leveraging Meta AI's image generation capabilities.
  • Meta-Specific Insights: Leverage insights from chat data on WhatsApp/Messenger for customer relationship management.

Gemini (Google)

Google's multimodal AI model, designed to be highly versatile and integrate seamlessly across Google services (Workspace, Cloud). It can process and understand various types of information, including text, images, audio, and video.

Relevant Sales Use Cases:
  • Comprehensive Research: Quickly synthesize information from documents, emails, and online research for deep customer insights and competitive analysis.
  • Content Creation across Workspace: Draft personalized emails, proposals, and presentations directly within Gmail, Docs, and Slides. Generate custom visuals for presentations.
  • Data Analysis & Forecasting: Analyze sales data in Sheets to identify trends, predict performance, and generate actionable insights.
  • Meeting Productivity: Summarize long emails, create meeting agendas, and transcribe/summarize meeting notes from Google Meet.
  • Lead Generation & Qualification: Analyze market trends and customer profiles to identify qualified leads.

Perplexity AI

An "answer engine" AI model that combines language model capabilities with real-time web search. It excels at providing direct, sourced answers by pulling and synthesizing information from the internet and citing its sources.

Relevant Sales Use Cases:
  • Real-time Market Research: Quickly find up-to-date information on market trends, competitor pricing, and feature sets, complete with citations.
  • Deep Prospect Research: Analyze industry news, company reports (PDFs), and online discussions to gather comprehensive insights on prospects.
  • Objection Handling Prep: Research common objections for specific industries or products by accessing current discussions and FAQs online.
  • Content Validation: Verify facts or statistics used in sales pitches and collateral by cross-referencing live web sources.
  • Targeted Sales Pitches: Helps create tailored sales pitches by analyzing prospect data and current market conditions.

ChatGPT (OpenAI)

A highly versatile conversational AI model from OpenAI, known for its strong natural language understanding and generation capabilities. It excels at creative writing, brainstorming, and engaging in natural dialogue.

Relevant Sales Use Cases:
  • Content & Communication Drafts: Generate diverse sales content, including cold outreach messages, follow-up emails, sales scripts, and social media captions.
  • Brainstorming & Idea Generation: Brainstorm ideas for campaigns, objection handling strategies, or new product features.
  • Customer Service Automation: Power chatbots for initial customer queries, providing immediate responses and qualifying leads.
  • Role-Playing: Simulate conversations for sales training and practice objection handling scenarios.
  • Summarization: Summarize long call notes or complex documents into concise formats.
